Door & Window Sensor

Automatic Trigger

Set up an automatic trigger on the Koogeek app to control other Koogeek accessories. For example, when you open the door at a selected time, the bedroom light will be automatically turned on.

Works with Koogeek Home app/ Apple Home app

Easy Installation

Advanced Security

HomeKit security communications are encrypted between a HomeKit-enabled accessory and your iPhone, iPad, or iPod touch.

Simple Battery Replacement

Monitor Several Sensors

Install more than one sensor in different places and name them respectively. Then all the statuses of doors / windows will be displayed simultaneously on your phone screen.

Remote Access

You can set up your Apple TV (4th generation or later with tvOS 10.1 or later) or iPad (running iOS 10.1 or later) as a home hub and remotely check the status of your home at a glance.
