Wi-Fi Enabled Smart Light Strip

Support Siri Voice Control

Apple HomeKit technology provides an easy, secure way to turn your connected light strip on or off, set light colors or adjust light brightness using the Koogeek Home app and Siri on your iPhone, iPad, iPod touch, or Apple Watch.

Ultimately flexible

Shape the light strip to desired forms and use the adhesive tape on the back to attach it to most clean, dry and flat solid surface. It can flexibly contour around corners and highlight architectural features.

16 Million Colors

Offer 16 million colors to choose from and every color is dimmable in the Koogeek Home app, your can match the light to your mood and personalize your home.

Create Scenes

Create scenes and control multiple HomeKit-enabled light strips with different light colors and brightness at the same time. Turn a scene on or off by tapping it, setting a timer or just asking Siri.

Schedule On/Off

Set custom schedules and timers for a light strip or a scene to turn connected light strips on or off at a specific time automatically.

Simple Setup

Connect it to a USB power source. Configure it from the Koogeek Home app over a 2.4 GHz Wi-Fi network. No hub or bridge required.

Automate and Remotely Access

You can set up your Apple TV or iPad as a home hub to control your HomeKit accessories remotely, grant access, and automate your accessories.
