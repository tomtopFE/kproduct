Wearable Smart Baby Thermometer

Hassle-free Wireless Connection

Turn on the Bluetooth function and the Koogeek app on your phone, and the thermometer will connect to your phone automatically. No password needed. You can monitor a fever more than 33-feet away.

Easy to Use

Place it under the armpit with an adhesive gel patch, connect it to the Koogeek app via wireless 4.0, and you can monitor your baby’s temperature on your phone. Get alerts when the temperature rises beyond the threshold.

Come with the medical double sided patch.

Shake the thermometer 3 times to turn it on or off.

The probe is flexible and toxic free.

High Accuracy

Get a highly accurate reading of the baby’s temperature in 2 minutes, with the deviation of ±0.05℃. More precise than a conventional medical thermometer.

6 Months Replaceable Battery

Powered by one CR2032 3V / 200mAh cell battery, which can be replaced.

TI CC2541 Chip

Store up to 7 days of temperature information. Export measurement readings to the Apple Health app whenever you want.
